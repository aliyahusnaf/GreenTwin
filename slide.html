EcoGrid Vietnam : AI-Powered 3D Urban Energy & Mobility Intelligence Platform
A shared AI intelligence layer — closing Vietnam's developer↔government coordination gap on renewable energy & low-carbon mobility
Solution Description
Vietnam's low-carbon transition is trapped in a structural coordination deadlock: EV sales surged 2.5× in 2024, yet 18% of would-be EV buyers abandon the purchase due to insufficient chargers (FTU HCMC, 2024); Vietnam needs USD 12B in EVSE investment (HSBC) that won't materialize without coordination. EcoGrid resolves this as a shared AI intelligence layer with a closed feedback loop: developer plans → city energy forecast; policy simulation → recommends where to prioritize EVSE & solar (e.g. EV demand surge in District X → platform flags that district for EVSE deployment). The 3D Digital Twin is not decoration — it is the common language allowing a rooftop solar decision in District 1 to be understood in the context of mobility policy in District 3.

How It Works — Two Integrated Modules + Digital Twin
MODULE 1 — Smart Infrastructure Planning
For: Developers · EVSE Operators · ESG Investors
Sub-Feature 1A — Grid Stability, BESS Sizing & EVSE Placement
Ingests EVN historical load data → computes proposed building peak demand vs. local transformer capacity → auto-sizes optimal BESS (kWh + cost estimate) to prevent grid overload. Cable loss minimization (∑I²·R·L) finds optimal EV charging pile positions inside parking structure. 3D underground X-ray view shows existing cable trenches & transformer tie-in points — no trial-and-error surveys. Supports Vietnam's 150,000 EVSE target by 2030.
Sub-Feature 1B — Solar Rooftop Optimization
Full annual ray-casting simulation (Solargis Vietnam GHI): casts shadow volumes from neighbor buildings hour-by-hour → pinpoints best-yield rooftop zones. Drag-and-drop panel placement gives live AI feedback: annual kWh yield, CO₂ offset, payback period. Directly advances Vietnam's 39.2% renewable energy target by 2045.
Output: Energy Efficiency & Grid Readiness Certificate (ESG-ready)

MODULE 2 — Low-Carbon Mobility Policy Simulation
For: Depts. of Transport · City Planning · MoIT · EVN
⚡ Urgent use case: Hanoi Directive 20
Gasoline motorbike ban: Ring Road 1 (Jul 2026) → Ring Road 2 (2028) → Ring Road 3 (2030). 6.9M motorbikes affected. Two-wheelers meet 72.6% of Hanoi's transport needs (ICCT, 2022) — largest urban mobility transition in Asia.
Agent-Based Modeling (ABM)
Simulates 10,000s of synthetic citizens (calibrated GSO 2023 household survey). Apply Ring Road ban → predicts who shifts to e-moto, BRT, private EV, or faces mobility exclusion (equity flag).
Emission Physics Engine + RE Demand Forecaster
Computes CO₂, NOx, PM2.5 changes (IPCC Tier 2 / MoNRE). Calculates additional green electricity needed per scenario. Critical flag: does the EV transition actually reduce emissions, or merely shift them from exhaust pipes to coal plants? — answered explicitly per scenario.
3D Pollution Fog + XAI Policy Reports
Live volumetric PM2.5 fog layer thins as policy sliders move (zone, timeline, EV subsidy). Claude API generates plain-language XAI report: why emissions fell, who benefits, who is at risk.
Output: XAI Policy Report + RE Demand Forecast per Scenario
